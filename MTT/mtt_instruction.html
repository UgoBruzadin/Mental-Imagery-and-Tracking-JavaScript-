Instructions: Manual Tracking Task

This is a test of your ability to track a circle with your mouse.
Four (4) targets will show on the screen for 3 seconds.
When targets are gone, one circle will show on the right OR on the left of the screen.
The circle will zig-zag, bouncing from where the 4 targets were.
Your objective is to track the movement of the circle with the mouse as best as you can.

On half the trials, a gray rectangle will cover the center of the screen.

Do not use your hands or fingers to help.
This is a memory task.
Use only the mouse pointer to track the circle.

If you don't move your mouse too much, the trial will be considered a "miss".
If you miss three trials, this experiment will be terminated.

First, there will be a training session. Get used to the size of the circle.
Your objective is to keep the mouse as close to the center of the circle as possible.

When you're ready to start, click "Start Training"
